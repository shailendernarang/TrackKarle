TrackKarle – Privacy Policy (Text Version)
Last updated: 8 Nov 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how TrackKarle ("the App", "we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, and shares information when you use the App.

App name: TrackKarle
Package: com.ss.wealthtracker
Advertising: Google Mobile Ads (AdMob)

1) Information We Collect
- No account or personal profile is required by the App.
- Local-only app data: your investments and settings are stored on your device. We do not operate servers to receive or store your content.
- Platform/diagnostic data: Google Play and/or your device OS may collect limited diagnostic information per their policies (e.g., crash logs, ANRs, app version).
- Advertising data (via AdMob SDK): device information such as IP address, Advertising ID, approximate location (derived from IP), app interactions (ad impressions/clicks), and other signals necessary for ads and fraud prevention.

2) Advertising (AdMob)
- The App displays banner ads using Google Mobile Ads SDK (AdMob).
- AdMob may process data for ad delivery, measurement, and fraud prevention.
- Learn more:
  • Google Ads & AdMob Privacy: https://policies.google.com/technologies/ads
  • How Google uses info from apps: https://policies.google.com/technologies/partner-sites

3) Children’s Privacy
- The App is not directed to children under 13 (or under the age required by local law, e.g., 16 in the EEA/UK). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.
- If you believe we have collected information from a child, contact us so we can take appropriate action.

4) Permissions Used
- INTERNET: required for loading ads and fetching external content such as news feeds.
- ACCESS_NETWORK_STATE: used to check network connectivity for ads and content fetching.
- Any additional permissions added in future releases will be disclosed in the Play listing and reflected in this policy.

5) Data Storage and Security
- Your financial data (e.g., investments, settings) is stored locally on your device (e.g., Room database or SharedPreferences).
- We recommend using a device screen lock and keeping your OS/apps updated for maximum security.

6) Data Retention
- Local app data remains on your device until you delete it or uninstall the App.
- Ad-related data is processed and retained by Google in accordance with Google’s policies (see links above).

7) Your Choices and Controls
- Reset your Advertising ID and/or limit ad personalization in Android system settings.
- Manage Google ad settings at: https://adssettings.google.com
- If required by law (e.g., EEA/UK), a consent dialog (Google’s User Messaging Platform) may request your consent for personalized ads. You can revisit and change preferences through that consent dialog when shown by the SDK.
- To stop all data collection, uninstall the App.

8) Your Rights (GDPR/EEA/UK)
- Where applicable, you may have rights to: access, rectify, erase, restrict or object to processing of your personal data, and data portability.
- To exercise your rights regarding data processed by Google for ads, use Google’s tools/links above and contact Google as described in their policies. For App-local data, you can delete data or uninstall the App. You may also contact us (see Contact Us).

9) California Privacy (CCPA/CPRA)
- We do not sell or share personal information as defined by CCPA/CPRA.
- You may limit ad personalization in Android settings and via applicable consent dialogs.
- To exercise rights under CCPA/CPRA, contact us (see Contact Us) and use Google’s privacy tools referenced above.

10) Third-Party SDKs
- Google Mobile Ads SDK (AdMob). If we add more SDKs in future, we will update this section.

11) International Users
- Ad services (e.g., Google AdMob) may process data on servers worldwide. Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries outside your own.

12) Changes to This Policy
- We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes in the App, SDKs, or legal requirements. The “Last updated” date indicates the latest revision.
