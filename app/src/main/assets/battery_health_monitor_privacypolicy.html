Privacy Policy

Last updated: 16 Mar 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Battery Health Monitor ("the App", "we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, and shares information when you use the App.

  • App name: Battery Health Monitor
  • Package: com.ss.batteryhealthmonitor
  • Analytics: Firebase Analytics, Crashlytics & Performance Monitoring

1) Information We Collect

  • No account or personal profile: The App does not require you to create an account or provide direct identifiers.
  • Local-only data: Your battery history, settings, and preferences are stored locally on your device. We do not run servers to receive or store your content.
  • Battery data: The App reads battery level, temperature, voltage, and current from your device's system APIs. This data is stored locally and never transmitted to our servers.
  • Crash/performance data (Firebase): Firebase Crashlytics collects crash reports and Firebase Performance collects app performance metrics to help us improve stability. These may include device model, OS version, and stack traces. No personal data is included.
  • Analytics (Firebase): Firebase Analytics collects anonymous usage data such as screen views and feature usage to help us understand how the App is used. No personally identifiable information is collected.

2) Firebase Services

The App uses Firebase SDKs provided by Google. Firebase may collect and process device information such as device model, OS version, app version, and anonymous usage statistics.

Learn more:

  • Firebase Privacy & Security
  • Google Privacy Policy

3) Permissions

The App requests the following permissions:

  • INTERNET & ACCESS_NETWORK_STATE: Required for Firebase services (crash reporting, analytics, performance monitoring).
  • POST_NOTIFICATIONS: To send battery alerts and health notifications.
  • FOREGROUND_SERVICE: To run background battery monitoring.
  • RECEIVE_BOOT_COMPLETED: To restart battery monitoring after device reboot.

4) Children's Privacy

The App is not directed to children under 13 (or under the age required by local law). If you believe we have collected information from a child, please contact us to take appropriate action.

5) Data Storage and Security

Your battery data is stored locally on your device (SharedPreferences and Room database). We recommend keeping your device secure with a screen lock and OS updates. No battery data is transmitted to external servers.

6) Your Choices and Controls

  • Firebase Analytics: You can opt out of Firebase Analytics data collection via Android's device settings under Google > Ads.
  • Notifications: You can disable notifications in Android system settings for the App.
  • Data export: You can export your battery data as CSV from within the App.
  • Uninstall: You can stop all data collection by uninstalling the App. All local data is deleted upon uninstall.

7) Third-Party Links

The App may contain links to third-party websites or services not operated by us. We are not responsible for their content or privacy practices.

8) International Users

Firebase services (Google) may process data on servers worldwide. Anonymous analytics and crash data may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your own.

9) Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes in the App or applicable laws. The "Last updated" date indicates the latest revision.
